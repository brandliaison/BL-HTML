Guidelines for implementation of IS 302 (Part 1):2024 /IEC 60335-1:2020

2024-11-27

The Bureau of Indian Standards (BIS) has revised the standard IS 302 (Part 1): 2024 / IEC 60335-1: 2020 which will replace IS 302-1: 2008 for “Adapters for Household and Similar Electrical Appliances” under mandatory certification (Scheme II). The last date for compliance with the same is 23rd February 2025, after which the existing standard IS 302-1: 2008 will be withdrawn.


Key changes brought through this revision:
  • Added safety requirements as covered by the IEC 60335 series and how to retrieve them.
  • Introduced new testing and component standards such as mechanical strength, humidity tests, and moisture resistance for various appliances.
  • Provided updated requirements for battery-operated products, plug-in appliances, and lithium-ion batteries, along with other appliances and components covered under the existing IS 302-1: 2008.
Guidelines for:
Existing licensees:

Must conform to the revised IS 302 (Part 1): 2024 / IEC 60335-1: 2020 by 23rd February 2025, submit compliance test reports provided by BIS accredited labs, and provide an undertaking of compliance of models under the revised standard, failure to which will initiate cancellation of license.

New applicants:

New applications for the older standard are accepted only until 23rd January 2025, given there is an undertaking provided to conform and transition to the newly revised standards by 23rd February 2025, beyond which no new application will be accepted for the older standard.

Change of scope:

Changes are allowed in the scope only before the transition to the revised standard or till implementation of the deadline.

This notification is in immediate effect and non-compliance will result in license suspension. Contact Brand Liaison for more info regarding this Notification.
