Challenges in Product Certification and How Brand Liaison Helps Solve Them

Posted on 18/02/2025
0

The global market is now expanding at an exponential rate as there is not just an increase in the number of established businesses and startups, but also an increasing variety of diverse goods and articles. By exploring the pool of options available in the market, consumers can choose products that suit their requirements the most and also support businesses.

However, staying compliant with market regulations is essential for all businesses. There are various certification requirements, based on the type of products, and the market requirement for the same. For instance, a BIS certificate is needed to ensure the quality and safety of products, whereas TEC registration is mandatory for telecommunication equipment in India. Similarly, a BEE star rating certificate is essential for energy-consuming appliances while WPC ETA approval is needed for products that have wireless-based technology.

But abiding by these mandatory compliances also incorporates certain challenges for businesses. Overcoming the many overwhelming product certification challenges is the need of the hour, to manage compliance requirements in the stringent Indian economy.

Let us have a look at why product compliance solutions are important, what are the common challenges businesses face on an everyday basis, and how Brand Liaison helps in solving them.

Benefits of Abiding by the Market Compliances in India

There are many benefits that producers, importers, and businesses gain by abiding by the market regulations in the Indian market.

  • Businesses gain an advantage in the competitive economy, by staying compliant with the market regulations.
  • The compliance with the mandatory certifications is a legal requirement and skipping the same can lead to many legal actions such as fines and penalties.
  • The product compliance solutions help businesses explore and access various market opportunities and also expand their growth over time.
  • Producers, importers, and business owners get a market edge over their competitors by following the legal requirements and abiding with the product certification regulations.
  • Consumer confidence is established in the market and people can trust the businesses. They can make informed decisions in the market while exploring a range of options.

Common Challenges Faced During Product Certifications

There are many challenges that businesses have to face and simultaneously overcome in the stringent Indian market. Businesses should not skip these certification requirements as there is a risk of legal fines and penalties on non-conformance.

Evolving Regulations and Updates

There are always regular updates and amendments in various compliance requirements that must be followed by businesses in order to never lag in the soaring competition. However, keeping track of all the updates and amendments, or even new regulations, can cause a hassle for businesses in the long run.
